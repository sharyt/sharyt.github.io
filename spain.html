Barcelona is an especially beautiful city. You have the grandeur of the gothic architecture, but the ease of a beach town. It is also the capital of Catalonia, whose people have made visible efforts to keep their rich culture and identity. One of the big standout moments of experiencing Barcelona was being able to witness a full band perform in front of the Barcelona Catherdal to a huge group of Catalans who danced the Sardana - a tradition native to Catalonia. It goes without saying, that the dance is beautiful, and if you happen to be more talented in the dancing department than yours truly, you should definitely give it a try.
